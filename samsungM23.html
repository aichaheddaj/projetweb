Product Details

The Galaxy M23 5G is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 700 processor, which provides efficient performance and 5G connectivity. It comes with 4GB or 6GB of RAM and 64GB or 128GB of internal storage, which can be expanded with a microSD card. The phone features a quad-camera setup on the back, including a 48-megapixel main camera, a 5-megapixel ultra-wide camera, a 2-megapixel macro camera, and a 2-megapixel depth sensor. It also has a 13-megapixel front-facing camera for selfies and video calls. Other features of the Samsung Galaxy M23 5G include a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, a 5,000mAh battery with support for 15W fast charging, and Samsung's One UI 3.1 based on Android 11. It is available in two colors: black and blue.
